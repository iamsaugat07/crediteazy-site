CreditEazy

A simple, offline-first mobile app to track customer credit and debit ledgers. Built for small businesses in Nepal.

Features

Customer Ledgers

Track credit and debit balances for each customer with a clear transaction history.

Sales & Payments

Record sales and payments easily. See outstanding balances at a glance.

Offline First

All data is stored locally on your device. No internet required for daily use.

Google Drive Backup

Optionally back up your data to Google Drive for safekeeping and restore on a new device.

Bilingual

Full support for English and Nepali languages with Nepali numeral formatting.

PDF Reports

Generate and share customer ledger reports as PDF documents.
